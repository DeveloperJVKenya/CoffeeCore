Delete Your CoffeeCore Account & Data

CoffeeCore · Package: com.jvalmacis.coffeecore · Published by JV Alma C.I.S

You can request permanent deletion of your CoffeeCore account and all data associated with it at any time. This page explains what gets deleted, how long it takes, and how to submit a request.

To request deletion, email us from the address linked to your account:
infojvalmacis@gmail.com
Use the subject line: "CoffeeCore Account Deletion Request" and include the email address or username registered on your account.

What we delete

  • Your account profile (name, email address, phone number)
  • Farm, field, and crop-cycle records you created (mapping data, soil records, yield and finance entries)
  • Pest and disease intervention history logged under your account
  • Saved preferences, reminders, and in-app messages
  • Any other personal data linked to your account in our systems

How the process works

  1. Send a deletion request to infojvalmacis@gmail.com from the email address on your account (or state the registered username/phone if you signed up another way).
  2. We verify the request belongs to the account owner.
  3. We permanently delete the account and associated personal data described above within 7 business days of verification.
  4. You'll receive an email confirmation once deletion is complete.

Data we may retain

Some information may be retained for a limited period where required by law, for fraud prevention, or to resolve disputes (for example, transaction records required for accounting/tax compliance). Retained data is kept only as long as legally necessary and is not used for any other purpose.

Partial deletion

If you'd like specific data deleted (for example, a single farm record) without closing your account entirely, mention this in your email and we'll process a partial deletion instead.
